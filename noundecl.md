## Declensions

Note: word counts do not include words that are in multiple declensions (above).

Examples are in nominative singular, genitive singular/plural, partitive singular/plural and illative singular/plural.
Genitive plurals that end with *-in* or *-itten* have been omitted.
*V* = any vowel, *C* = any consonant, *A* = *a* or *ä*, *O* = *o* or *ö*, *U* = *u* or *y*.
